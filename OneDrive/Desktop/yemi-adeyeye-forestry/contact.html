Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about collaboration and services

What consulting services do you offer?

I offer consulting services in urban forestry planning, environmental governance, stakeholder engagement, and natural resource management. This includes strategic planning, policy development, and implementation guidance for municipalities and organizations.

Are you available for international projects?

Yes, I have extensive experience working on international projects across five continents. I'm available for both remote consultation and on-site engagements depending on project requirements.

What topics do you speak about?

My speaking topics include urban forestry leadership, environmental governance, youth engagement in sustainability, ecosystem restoration, climate resilience, and multi-stakeholder collaboration in natural resource management.

How can we collaborate on research?

I'm open to research collaborations with academic institutions, NGOs, and government agencies. Areas of interest include environmental governance, Indigenous knowledge systems, urban forestry innovation, and youth engagement in environmental initiatives.
