# Organization Logos

This directory contains logos for the organizational affiliations displayed on the website.

## Required Logo Files

Please add the following logo images to this directory:

1. **fao-logo.png** - Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO)
2. **un-logo.png** - United Nations logo
3. **cifor-icraf-logo.png** - CIFOR-ICRAF (Center for International Forestry Research - World Agroforestry)
4. **glf-logo.png** - Global Landscapes Forum logo
5. **iufro-logo.png** - International Union of Forest Research Organizations (IUFRO)
6. **ypard-logo.png** - Young Professionals for Agricultural Development (YPARD)
7. **windsor-logo.png** - City of Windsor logo
8. **expertise-france-logo.png** - Expertise France logo
9. **sdc-logo.png** - Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation (SDC)

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or SVG
- **Size**: Recommended 300-400px width, maintain aspect ratio
- **Background**: Transparent background preferred
- **Color**: Full color logos work best
- **Naming**: Use lowercase with hyphens (as listed above)

## Fallback Behavior

If a logo image is not found or fails to load, the website will automatically display a fallback icon instead. This ensures the page always looks good even if some logos are missing.

## Where to Find Logos

- Organization official websites (usually in "Media" or "Press" sections)
- Organization brand guidelines pages
- Wikipedia pages (check for official logos)
- Organization social media profiles

## Notes

- Ensure you have permission to use these logos
- Logos should be official versions from the organizations
- Maintain aspect ratios when resizing
