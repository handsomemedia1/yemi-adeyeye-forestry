About Dr. Adeyeye

A journey through forests, communities, and sustainable futures

Cultivating Forests, Building Communities

Dr. Yemi Adeyeye is an accomplished forestry and natural resource professional with over a decade of experience spanning advisory roles with international organizations, management of multi-stakeholder initiatives, and interdisciplinary research activities.

Currently serving as City Forester and Manager of Forestry & Natural Areas in Ontario, Canada, Dr. Adeyeye leads the strategic development of urban forest management plans valued at over $400 million. His work bridges the gap between scientific research, policy development, and community engagement.

With a PhD from the University of British Columbia and experience across five continents, Dr. Adeyeye brings a unique global perspective to local environmental challenges. His expertise encompasses urban forestry leadership, environmental governance, ecosystem restoration, and youth engagement in sustainable development.
